Applications

Virtual organs are more than conceptual apparatus - they are working apparatus, already re-inventing both medicine and research. By recreating organ function in a controlled and safe atmosphere, clinicians, researchers and students are able to safely evaluate therapies, research disease, and build medical devices without the risk of endangering the lives of patients...
